Bootstrap Glyphicons

Bootstrap provides 260 glyphicons from the Glyphicons Halflings set.

Glyphicons can be used in text, buttons, toolbars, naviation, forms, etc.

Glyphicon Syntax


  /span class="glyphicon glyphicon-name"/span
